Introduction

Welcome to HealthMate, your healthy friend. This guide will help you understand how to use our platform to manage your medical records efficiently.

HealthMate is a tool for understanding. Always consult a real doctor for medical decisions.

How to Upload Reports

You can upload images (JPG, PNG) or PDF files of your lab reports. Our system processes them directly.

  1. Go to your Dashboard.
  2. Click on "Analyze Report".
  3. Select your file and wait for your Dost to read it.

Reading Insights

Once processed, you will see a summary in two languages:

  • English: For technical accuracy.
  • Roman Urdu: For easy understanding at home.

We highlight abnormal values like High Sugar or Low Hemoglobin so you can discuss them with your doctor.

Tracking Vitals

Even without a lab report, you can keep a log of your health. Simply enter your Blood Pressure, Sugar levels, or Weight manually in the "Vitals" section.
